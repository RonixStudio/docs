  <Tab title="Installation">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Step 1">
        If you purchased the product, download the resource from **BuiltByBit** or **Polymart**. Confirm your **Tebex Plus** subscription is active. Download the zip package containing:

        - `Template/` (HTML/Twig files)\\
        - `Assets/` (images, JS, CSS dependencies)\\
        - `Schema.json` (settings)\\
        - `Theme/` (CSS styling file)

        <Note>
          You must have a Tebex Plus plan.
        </Note>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 2">
        <img
          src="/vio11.png"
          alt="Vio1 Pn"
          title=""
          style={{ width:"100%" }}
        />

        In Tebex, navigate to `Webstore` → `Appearance` → `Templates`.\
        Click `Change Template` → `Create Custom Template`.\
        Name your template (e.g., `Vio_Template`), select the `Exo` base, click `Create`.\
        \
        Open the editor:

        - Paste all `*.twig` files from Template/.
        - Upload `Schema.json` via the editor’s “Schema” panel.
        - Upload **everything** in Assets/ via the “Assets” button.

        Click **Save** & **Publish**.

        <Warning>
          Do not use “Preview” as it may display unstyled content.
        </Warning>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 3">
        ![Vio22 Pn](/images/vio22.png)

        Navigate to `Webstore` → `Appearance` → `Themes`.\
        Click `Create Custom Theme`, name it `Vio_Theme`.\
        Paste the **CSS** from `Theme/style.css`.\
        Click **Create**, then **Activate** the theme.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 4">
        Link your Discord account in **BuiltByBit** post-purchase. Join our **Discord** server and use **/sync** in any channel to **verify** your account. Open a **support ticket** to **activate the license** and receive setup instructions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Step 5">
        Ensure **both** template and theme are **active**. Clear your **browser cache** after changes to avoid outdated display.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  <Tab title="FAQ">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Why doesn’t my template show styling?">
        Ensure you uploaded: - schema.json in template - style.css in theme and activated it. Clear your cache if issues persist.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How to update Vio?">
        Download latest package.\
        Replace all Template/ and Theme/ files except config.twig (if you’ve customized settings).\
        Re-upload new schema.json.\
        Re-paste CSS to theme editor and click Save/Activate.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="What common mistakes occur?">
        Not uploading schema → missing setup options.\
        Using “Preview” instead of “Save & Publish”.\
        Forgetting to activate theme after upload.
      </Accordion>
